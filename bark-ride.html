Welcome to the Bark and Ride!

About Us

At the Bark and Ride, we believe that your pooch should have a fun place to spend their day while their parental unit is at work. We encourage you to check out the site more to see if this is a place for you and your four-legged friend to play during your day.

Features of our park:

  • Dog-friendly Pool
  • Chuck-it/Frizbee field
  • Grooming Station (Donations Welcome!)
  • Hydration / Feeding Stations
  • Relax and Sleep Area

What should I bring?

  1. Your dog!
  2. Any special food your furry friend may require
  3. Any special blanket/toy/play thing that Fido will want during his stay
